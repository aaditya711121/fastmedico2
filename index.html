Our Story

Healing Lives With
Science & Compassion

Founded in 2008, Fast Medico began with a single vision: to make high-quality healthcare accessible, affordable, and human. What started as a small general practice has grown into a multi-specialty clinic trusted by over 12,000 patients across Mumbai.

We believe exceptional medical care is both a science and an art. Our doctors don't just treat symptoms — they invest in understanding each patient as a whole person, building lasting relationships built on trust and transparency.
